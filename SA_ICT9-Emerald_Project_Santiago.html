Steps to prevent fire at home

How to prevent a fire
1.Unplug electrical appliances and turn off lights before leaving the house. Leaving the electrical appliances on will leave them at no attention and might cause them to short circuit while your gone and cause the house to burn down.
2.Dont do octopus wiring octopus wiring is very hazardous and can also electrocute you. When it sets on fire dont use water to set the fire off use a fire extinguisher or a piece of cloth as long as you dont use an electric conducter to put the fire out.
3.Dont ignite candles unless necessary. Dont ignite it near flammable items like paper or cloth and dont ignite candles near strong winds. When this happens dont use a piece of cloth to put the fire out but use water or a fire extinguisher.
4.Dont leave the kitchen unattended while cooking your food could be set on fire and get worse. When this happens set the fire off with water or a fire extinguisher
5.Get an electrician to inspect your light bulbs, electrical outlets, and your water heater. The electrician can find out if it is damaged.
